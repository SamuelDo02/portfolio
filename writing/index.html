"The world breaks everyone, and afterward, some are strong at the broken places."

- Ernest Hemingway in “A Farewell to Arms”

 I aim to resonate with the experience of emotional pain, which shrouds us in loneliness despite being an inextricable aspect of the human condition. Enduring life’s challenges helps us grow and transform, but without shared understanding, we risk being consumed by despair. Through my writing, I hope to provide empathetic insight to those who are grappling with pain, and I want to offer guidance and support as they embark on their journey towards healing and renewal.

 Below is a compilation of my current works. Some of these serve to embrace emotions and feel solidarity, and others are more focused on learning from emotions and processing them in new perspectives. My thoughts are intended for all audiences regardless of how much one relates to a situation.
